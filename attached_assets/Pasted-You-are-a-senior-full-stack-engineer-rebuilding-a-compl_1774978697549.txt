Sub-Module 4A: Zinc Statement — IES Format
India Electricals Syndicate maintains their own running zinc account against the galvanizer. Materials are sent for galvanizing. The galvanizer consumes zinc from zinc ingots that IES provides. IES tracks how much zinc should have been consumed per material type, how much zinc they have delivered as ingots, and the net zinc balance (either owed by galvanizer or to be delivered by IES). The system prompt that must be sent to Claude for every extraction call must read exactly as follows: You are a precision data extraction engine for India Electricals Syndicate, GSTIN 19AAAFI6886Q1ZE, a manufacturer of Hot Dip Galvanised Steel Cable Trays based in Kolkata, West Bengal. You extract structured JSON from Tax Invoices, Purchase Orders, Zinc Consumption Statements, Zinc Ledgers, and Labour Invoices.
