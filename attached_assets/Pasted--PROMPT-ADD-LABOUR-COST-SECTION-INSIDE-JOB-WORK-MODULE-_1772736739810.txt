# LABOUR COST

Purpose:
Track vendor-wise labour invoices raised for galvanisation work and compute payable amount after TDS adjustment.

This section is independent of Zinc Ledger but linked vendor-wise and vehicle-wise.

---

# STRUCTURE

Create a new subsection inside Job Work:

1. Incoming (Zinc Tracking)
2. Labour Cost (New Section)

---

# LABOUR COST — CORE LOGIC

Labour Cost is calculated based on:

Vendor-wise
Material Received (in kg)
×
Cost per kg charged by vendor

However, invoice amount shared by vendor is:

Total Amount (Inclusive of GST)

System must reverse-calculate and apply TDS.

---

---

# CALCULATION ENGINE

Invoice shared by vendor = Total Amount (Inclusive of GST)

System must:

Step 1: Identify GST % from invoice (12% or 18% typically)

Step 2: Reverse-calculate Basic Amount
Basic = Total / (1 + GST%)

Step 3: Calculate TDS
TDS = 2% of Basic Amount

Step 4: Final Payable Amount
Payable = Total Invoice Amount – TDS

Important Rule:
GST is calculated on full Basic.
TDS is deducted only on Basic.
GST is NOT reduced due to TDS.

Store:

* Basic Amount
* GST %
* GST Amount
* TDS (2%)
* Net Payable
* Gross Invoice Amount

All values stored with configurable decimal precision.
